Important clinical note

• In patients with burns >15% total body surface area, protein requirements may be significantly higher, even in the presence of chronic kidney disease.

• In patients with obesity (BMI >30 kg/m²), consider using ideal body weight or adjusted body weight for calorie targets, while maintaining a hypocaloric / high-protein approach per ASPEN / ESPEN guidance.

• Enteral formula suggestions are for guidance only and do not replace clinical judgment. Consider the need for modular protein supplementation if a protein deficit persists.
