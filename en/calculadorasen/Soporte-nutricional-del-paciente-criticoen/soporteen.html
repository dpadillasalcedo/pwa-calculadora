Important clinical note

• In patients with burns >15% total body surface area (TBSA), protein requirements may be significantly increased, even in the presence of chronic kidney disease.

• In patients with obesity (BMI >30 kg/m²), consider using ideal body weight or adjusted body weight for calorie estimation, while maintaining a hypocaloric / high-protein strategy according to ASPEN / ESPEN guidelines.

• Enteral formula suggestions are for guidance only and do not replace clinical judgment. Consider modular protein supplementation if a protein deficit persists.
